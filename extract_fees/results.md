# Mastercard Revises Digital Assurance Fee for Non-Tokenized Transactions

**Effective Date: 13 October 2025, Australia**

Mastercard is committed to enhancing the security and integrity of electronic commerce (e-commerce) transactions. As part of this initiative, we are revising the Digital Assurance Fee (DAF) for Non-Tokenized transactions. This change is aimed at fostering a safer e-commerce environment and ensuring the continued trust of our customers in the digital marketplace.

| Country | Effective Date | Fee Name                                                | Current Rate | New Rate |
|:--------|:---------------|:--------------------------------------------------------|--------------:|---------:|
| Australia | 2025-10-13   | Digital Assurance Acquirer Fee – Non-Tokenized (Debit)  | 0.01          | 0.04     |
| Australia | 2025-10-13   | Digital Assurance Acquirer Fee – Non-Tokenized (Credit) | 0.01          | 0.04     |
| Australia | 2025-10-13   | Digital Assurance Acquirer Fee – Non-Tokenized (Debit)  | 0.01          | 0.04     |
| Australia | 2025-10-13   | Digital Assurance Acquirer Fee – Non-Tokenized (Credit) | 0.01          | 0.04     |

We appreciate your understanding and cooperation as we implement this necessary fee adjustment. If you have any questions or require further clarification, please do not hesitate to contact us. We are here to assist you in navigating these changes and ensuring a smooth transition for your business operations.
