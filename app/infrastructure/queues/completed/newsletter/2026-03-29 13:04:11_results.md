# SwiftFreight Announces Revised Domestic Parcel Delivery Surcharges in Singapore

**Effective Date: 1 June 2025, Region: Asia / Pacific, Country: Singapore**

SwiftFreight, a leading global financial services company, is committed to providing efficient and reliable delivery services. In response to increased operational costs, including last-mile logistics and handling, SwiftFreight is revising the fees for domestic parcel delivery services in Singapore. The revised rates will apply to all domestic shipments billed weekly, effective 1 June 2025. SwiftFreight is dedicated to maintaining transparency and fairness in its pricing structure, and these changes reflect the company's ongoing efforts to ensure the sustainability of its services.

SwiftFreight is a trusted partner for shippers, merchants, and processors, offering a range of financial services that support global trade. The company is committed to providing high-quality services and products, and these fee adjustments are part of SwiftFreight's efforts to continue delivering value to its customers.

| Country | Effective Date | Fee Name | Current Rate | New Rate | Change Type |
|:-------|:---------------|:---------|--------------:|---------:|:------------|
| Singapore | 2025-06-01 | Standard Ground Delivery Surcharge | 2.6 | 2.75 | new_fee |
| Singapore | 2025-06-01 | Express Air Delivery Surcharge | 3.2 | 3.3 | new_fee |
| Singapore | 2025-06-01 | Residential Address Surcharge | 1.4 | 1.5 | new_fee |

SwiftFreight values its customers and is committed to providing exceptional service. If you have any questions or concerns regarding these changes, please do not hesitate to contact us. We appreciate your continued partnership and support.
